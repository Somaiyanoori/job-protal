About

Our Application Tracking feature streamlines the job application process for both job seekers and employers. For candidates, it provides a user-friendly interface to manage and monitor the status of their job applications in real-time, from submission to final decision. You can easily track where you stand in the hiring process, view updates, and receive notifications on interviews and feedback. For employers, the system offers an efficient way to organize and evaluate candidates, ensuring a seamless hiring workflow. The Application Tracking tool helps manage job postings, review applications, and communicate with potential hires—all in one place.
